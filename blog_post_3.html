Textbook & Paper Suggestions to Beginners

About Projects Blog

Here I aim to suggest some exceptional textbook and papers I found while trying to figure out how everything works. I will categorize them based on the field, however there might be some overlaps:

  • Reinforcement Learning
    • "Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction" by Richard Sutton & David Bartow
    • "Grokking Deep Reinforcement Learning" by Miguel Morales
    • "Algorithms for Decision Making" Mykel J. Kochenderfer & Tim Wheeler & Kyle Wray
    • Reinforcement Learning by Michael Littman & Charles Isbell
  • Optimization
    • "Convex Optimization" by Stephen Boyd and Lieven Vandenberghe
    • "Optimization Models" by Giuseppe Calafiore & Laurent El Ghaoui
    • "Algorithms for Optimization" Mykel J. Kochenderfer & Tim Wheeler
    • Convex Optimization by Stephen Boyd
  • Neural Networks
    • "Deep Learning" by Ian Goodfellow & Yoshua Bengio & Aaron Courville
    • Intro to Deep Learning with PyTorch by Luis Serrano
  • Linear Algebra
    • "Introduction to Linear Algebra" by Gilbert Strang
    • "Introduction to Applied Linear Algebra" by Stephen Boyd and Lieven Vandenberghe
